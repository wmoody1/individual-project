The Super Software Bros

The Super Software Bros are a team of four University of Memphis seniors who came together to complete this Software Engineering group project. Each member is involved in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department as well, through roles in both UofM RSO leadership and the IEEE Robot Team.

College Betting Pool | Software Engineering

This team project was to create a Django-based web application that automates the college betting pool at ReTrans. The process currently in place is manual and tedious, thus the purpose of the project is to provide an internet-capable process for as much of the “system” as possible.
